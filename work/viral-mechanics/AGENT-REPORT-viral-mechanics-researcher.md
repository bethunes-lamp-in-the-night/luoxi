## Key Findings

### Critical Success Factors

**1. First 3 Seconds = 80% of Viral Success**
- 65% of users decide to scroll or stay within 3 seconds
- Algorithm tracks "initial engagement rate" at this checkpoint
- **Luoxi application:** Grandmother photo (silent, 2-second pause before text) has highest retention potential

**2. Platform Algorithm Differences Matter**
- TikTok: Completion rate (40%) + Engagement velocity (30%) + Re-shares (20%)
- YouTube: Click-through rate (30%) + Watch time (25%) + Subscriber conversion (15%)
- Twitter: Engagement velocity in first 30 min determines algorithmic scaling
- **Implication:** Same content needs format adaptation, not just duplication

**3. Emotional Arc Timing is Algorithmically Optimal**
- Trust (0-15s) → Betrayal (15-35s) → Rage (35-50s) → Hope (50-60s)
- Pattern interrupts at 15s, 30s, 45s re-engage attention before drop-off
- **Luoxi application:** All 8 Western angles already structured for this arc

**4. The Luoxi Case Has Structural Viral Advantages**
- Documentary proof (autopsy contradicts diagnosis) = depoliticized credibility
- Iconic visual (grandmother feeding empty clothes at 2AM) = universal emotional impact
- Existing momentum (290K Douyin followers) = algorithm-friendly signals
- Censorship angle (deleted articles) = Western audience trigger
- **Compared to similar cases:** Superior evidence vs. Charlie Gard/Alfie Evans; comparable emotional impact; lower international presence (opportunity gap)
