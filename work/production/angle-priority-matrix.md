## Priority Ranking Methodology

**Scoring dimensions (1-10 scale):**
1. **Viral Potential** - Phase 3 algorithmic analysis score
2. **Production Difficulty** - Time/skill required (10=easiest, 1=hardest)
3. **Asset Readiness** - Photos/docs ready to use (10=ready, 1=needs work)
4. **Platform Fit** - Works on TikTok/IG Reels (primary Week 1 platforms)
5. **Emotional Impact** - Universal human resonance (transcends culture)

**Composite Score** = Average of 5 dimensions
**Priority** = Highest composite score = Week 1 production
