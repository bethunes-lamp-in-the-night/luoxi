# Platform Censorship Analysis - Luoxi Incident

**Analysis Date:** January 11, 2026
**Analyst:** Chinese Internet Researcher Agent

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Censorship of Luoxi incident content varies significantly by platform. **Xiaohongshu shows most aggressive suppression** (post burial, engagement manipulation). **Douyin appears most permissive** (family maintains 290K+ follower account with active posting). **WeChat Public Accounts experienced targeted deletions** of critical commentary articles. **Weibo shows moderate censorship** (selective post removal, engagement friction). This creates strategic opportunity: **use Douyin for grassroots mobilization** while **archiving everything immediately** before censorship escalates.

## CENSORSHIP EVASION TACTICS OBSERVED

### 1. Live Streaming (Douyin)
**Tactic:** Real-time video documentation
**Effectiveness:** HIGH - hard to censor in real-time
**Example:** Parents live-streaming hospital visits
**Risk:** Can be stopped mid-stream, not archived automatically

### 2. Celebrity Amplification
**Tactic:** High-profile accounts posting
**Effectiveness:** HIGH - deleting creates Streisand Effect
**Example:** Han Hong, He Sui, Wang Jinsong posts survived
**Risk:** Celebrities may face pressure to delete

### 3. Coded Language
**Tactic:** Using synonyms or phonetic equivalents
**Effectiveness:** MODERATE - delays detection
**Example:** Not widely observed in this case
**Risk:** Reduces searchability

### 4. Image Text
**Tactic:** Posting text as images to evade keyword filters
**Effectiveness:** MODERATE - OCR can detect
**Example:** Autopsy report screenshots
**Risk:** Less shareable, accessibility issues

### 5. Cross-Platform Redundancy
**Tactic:** Posting same content on multiple platforms
**Effectiveness:** MODERATE - if one deleted, others may survive
**Example:** Story spread across Douyin, Weibo, Zhihu
**Risk:** Coordinated deletion possible

### 6. Overseas Archiving
**Tactic:** CDT and others archiving deleted content
**Effectiveness:** HIGH - preserves for record
**Example:** 4 deleted articles archived
**Risk:** Archives outside China have limited reach inside China

### 7. Private Sharing
**Tactic:** WeChat groups, private messages
**Effectiveness:** UNKNOWN - inaccessible to research
**Example:** Likely extensive private sharing
**Risk:** No public record, hard to measure impact
