## MOST VALUABLE DISCOVERIES

### 1. **Active Censorship Pattern Documented**
China Digital Times archived 4 deleted articles including "患者不能成为试验品" (Patients Cannot Become Test Subjects). Hospital deleted official statement about autopsy on Dec 20, documented by commentator Hu Xijin. Xiaohongshu actively suppressing posts with manipulated engagement numbers. This creates urgency for Western platform amplification.

### 2. **Celebrity Viral Momentum (Nov 29 - Dec 20)**
International supermodel **He Sui** fired "first shot" Nov 29 with cryptic post supporting family. Singer **Han Hong** (major charity figure) called case "ridiculously wrong" Dec 20. Actor **Wang Jinsong** amplified. Family's Douyin account **@小洛熙妈妈** has **292,769 followers** - massive grassroots base for content amplification.

### 3. **Forensic Evidence Bombshell (Dec 19)**
Autopsy by respected forensic expert **Liu Liang** contradicted hospital records:
- No 7mm "coronary sinus type" defect existed (hospital's surgical justification)
- Only 3mm defect found (80%+ natural closure rate, no surgery needed)
- **6.5cm chest wound UNSUTURED**
- **5cm pericardial wound UNSUTURED**
- 70ml blood loss in chest cavity (50%+ of infant's total blood)
- Death from hemorrhagic shock + surgical malpractice

This is the **most egregious fact** for viral content - "baby bled to death from unsutured wounds."

### 4. **Systemic Hospital Issues Revealed**
NTD TV (overseas) reported **5 additional child deaths** at same hospital on operating tables, plus 5-year-old who died from IV drip. Pediatric cardiothoracic surgery department **permanently closed** Dec 22. Pattern suggests systemic problems beyond single incident - powerful narrative angle.

### 5. **Provincial-Level Political Response**
Zhejiang Provincial Party Committee dispatched work group Dec 23 (unusual high-level response). Chief surgeon Chen and anesthesia director dismissed. Hospital leaders disciplined. Indicates incident reached critical political threshold - shows campaign can force authority response.
