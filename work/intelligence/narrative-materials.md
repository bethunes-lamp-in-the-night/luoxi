## MOST SHOCKING AUTOPSY FINDINGS

### Visual/Documentary Evidence Priority

**1. NO CORONARY SINUS DEFECT FOUND**
- **Hospital claimed:** 7mm coronary sinus-type atrial septal defect requiring surgery
- **Autopsy found:** NO such defect exists
- **Actual finding:** Only 3mm secundum ASD (different, milder condition)
- **Source:** Vision Times, Zhihu (Source #015), autopsy analysis
- **Shock Factor:** MAXIMUM - Surgery was for disease that didn't exist
- **Narrative:** False diagnosis, unnecessary surgery, infant killed for fake disease
- **Visual:** Can be illustrated with before/after diagnosis comparison

**2. PERICARDIAL PATCH LEFT INSIDE HEART**
- **Medical records claimed:** "All patches were removed"
- **Autopsy found:** 1.4 × 0.5 cm pericardial patch still inside heart
- **Source:** Vision Times, Yangcheng Evening News (Source #050)
- **Shock Factor:** VERY HIGH - Direct falsification, foreign object left in baby
- **Narrative:** Falsified records, surgical negligence
- **Visual:** Can be illustrated (medical diagram of patch location)

**3. 6.5CM SURGICAL WOUND UNSUTURED**
- **Finding:** Large chest wound left open/improperly closed
- **Media description:** Baby "bled to death"
- **Source:** Yangcheng Evening News (#050), Tencent News (#006)
- **Shock Factor:** VERY HIGH - Basic surgical closure failure
- **Narrative:** Gross negligence, preventable death
- **Visual:** Graphic but powerful (use with content warning)

**4. 80% WOULD HEAL NATURALLY**
- **Autopsy finding:** 3mm secundum ASD
- **Medical fact:** "More than 80% healing naturally within a year" in infants
- **Expert opinion:** "Typically resolves on its own without surgery"
- **Source:** Vision Times
- **Shock Factor:** HIGH - Baby likely didn't need ANY surgery
- **Narrative:** Unnecessary procedure, killed healthy baby
- **Visual:** Can be illustrated with medical statistics graphic

**5. SURGERY DURATION: 3 HOURS → 6 HOURS**
- **Promised:** "Under 3 hours"
- **Actual:** 335 minutes (5.6 hours), total ordeal ~6 hours
- **Success rate promised:** 99.5%
- **Result:** Death
- **Source:** Autopsy report, Sixth Tone
- **Shock Factor:** HIGH - Massive complications vs. promised routine
- **Narrative:** Complications hidden, family misled
