## Critical Messaging Principles

### DO Say (Authority-Aligned):
- "Local officials betrayed Xi's 'people first' principles"
- "This demonstrates why anti-corruption campaign is essential"
- "We will honor Luoxi by creating systemic change"
- "Strong institutions hold individuals accountable"

### DON'T Say (Opposition Framing):
- "The government must be pressured to act" (implies weakness)
- "The Chinese system failed" (attacks legitimacy)
- "International pressure is needed" (implies external interference)

### Key Chinese Phrases to Use:
- "习近平总书记强调的'全面从严治党'精神" (Xi's comprehensive strict Party governance)
- "少数腐败分子" (a few corrupt elements) vs "系统性问题" (systemic problems)
- "人民至上、生命至上" (people first, life first - Xi quote)
- "为人民服务" (serve the people - foundational CCP principle)
