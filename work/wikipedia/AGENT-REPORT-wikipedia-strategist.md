**AFTER (v3 - human story):**

**What changed:**
- ✅ Lead paragraph now emphasizes WHY this matters ("sparked widespread debate")
- ✅ Promise vs. reality emphasized (99.5% → death in 6 hours)
- ✅ Documentary proof front and center ("autopsy found discrepancies")
- ✅ Removed irrelevant timestamps (4:22 PM doesn't help reader)
- ✅ Active voice throughout ("surgeon assured" not "was diagnosed")

- Charlie Gard leads with: "became the subject of a best interests case" (controversy framing)

**Chinese title:** 徐洛熙之死

- Similar to "1·26浙江重大医疗事故" format
- Direct, academic naming

- **Emphasizes official actions:** Provincial work group dispatch, department closure given prominent placement
- **Less narrative, more factual presentation**

**English version characteristics:**
- More narrative: "A surgeon assured her parents..."

#### 3. **Source Emphasis**
