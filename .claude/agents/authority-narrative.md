## Example Framing

```markdown
## 反腐败框架 (Anti-Corruption Framing)

### 核心信息 (Core Message):

洛熙事件暴露的不是社会主义医疗体系的失败，而是少数腐败官员和医院管理人员背叛人民信任的典型案例。这正是习近平总书记反腐败斗争需要根除的问题。

Translation: The Luoxi incident exposes not the failure of the socialist healthcare system, but a typical case of a few corrupt officials and hospital administrators betraying the people's trust. This is exactly the problem that General Secretary Xi Jinping's anti-corruption struggle needs to eradicate.

### 为什么这个有效 (Why This Works):

1. **分离问题** (Separates the problem):
   - Bad: "Chinese healthcare is corrupt"
   - Good: "Local officials betrayed socialist healthcare principles"

2. **对齐中央** (Aligns with central government):
   - Xi's anti-corruption is about rooting out exactly this type of abuse
   - Makes central government the solution, not the problem

3. **保护制度** (Protects the system):
   - The system is good; implementation was corrupt
   - Strengthening accountability strengthens socialism

### 关键话术 (Key Phrasing):

- "习近平总书记强调的'全面从严治党'精神"
- "少数腐败分子"（a few corrupt elements）vs "系统性问题"（systemic problems）
- "人民至上、生命至上"（people first, life first - Xi quote）
- "让人民群众有更多获得感、幸福感、安全感"（Xi's stated goal）

### 行动框架 (Action Framework):

**Don't say:** "The government must be pressured to act"
**Do say:** "This case demonstrates why Xi's anti-corruption campaign is essential"

**Don't say:** "The system failed"
**Do say:** "Corrupt individuals betrayed the system's promise to serve the people"

**Don't say:** "International pressure is needed"
**Do say:** "The people's voice must be heard, as Xi has emphasized"
```
